# NexusMind Design Context

## Color

Use tinted near-neutrals, not pure black or white. Preferred landing palette: deep ink `#111612`, porcelain green `#eef7ef`, signal coral `#d85f3f`, cyan proof `#0d8a96`, chlorophyll `#3f8c54`, and warm paper `#f8f5eb`.

## Typography

Use system UI fonts already available offline. Avoid remote font dependencies on marketing pages. Headings should use strong weight and tight but readable line height. Body copy should stay below 75 characters per line.

## Shape

Landing pages should use full-width narrative bands, asymmetric grids, product mockups, and inline proof. Cards are allowed for individual repeated items only. Avoid cards inside cards.

## Motion

Use purposeful reveal and hover motion only. Keep reduced-motion support. Avoid bounce and elastic easing.

## Components

Primary CTAs use compact rounded rectangles. Secondary CTAs use bordered buttons. Product visuals should show actual system states: risk, timeline, assessment, and admin actions.

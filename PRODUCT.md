# NexusMind Product Context

Register: brand

Users: mahasiswa, karyawan, admin kampus, dan pengelola organisasi yang perlu membaca risiko burnout tanpa menunggu keluhan menjadi krisis.

Product purpose: NexusMind mengubah asesmen, curhat anonim, prediksi machine learning, dan rekomendasi terapi menjadi satu alur pemantauan kesehatan mental yang bisa dipakai berulang.

Brand voice: calm, clinical, decisive, protective. The page should feel like a precise wellness commerce page: direct value, clear proof, and a strong path to start.

Anti-references: generic SaaS purple gradients, glass cards, oversized emoji, vague productivity claims, nested card grids, decorative dashboards that do not explain the product.

Strategic principles:
- Lead with the emotional cost of late burnout detection, then sell the relief of seeing signals earlier.
- Treat the landing page as conversion material: offer, proof, feature stack, workflow, trust, and final CTA.
- Keep the product credible: do not promise diagnosis, cure, or medical certainty.
- Make the first screen feel premium and operational, not like a brochure.
